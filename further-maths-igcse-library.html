About

The Further Maths IGCSE Library is a math/coding project created by Donald Leung. It was originally created at around October 2014, but was deleted during the major update that occurred during the 2015 New Year. After James Baghurst came to him and mentioned about the deleted webpage, he decided to re-create the Library. It was perfect timing because he just started to think about messing with JavaScript, a coding language that he wasn't good at.

Compatibility

The Further Maths IGCSE Library has been tested on Safari and Google Chrome. It works perfectly fine on Safari, though not so good on Google Chrome.
